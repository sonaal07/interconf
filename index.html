    Welcome to ICEBU 2016, Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, INDIA

    Dear Colleagues, ICEBU is an International Conference conceptualized by Department of Mechanical Engineering, Faculty of Engineering & Technology, Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, India. The services or utilities of the buildings anywhere in general is designed and conceptualized before the construction starts for and new project. Therefore, Architecture, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering all are coordinated. ICEBU will emerge as a well recognized and an eagerly anticipated event in the country because of it will have high quality technical sessions and the participation from various branches of Engineering, Consultants, Researchers, Contractors and Keynote Speakers both from various countries and from India. ,Jamia Millia Islamia in collaboration with and Edinburgh Napier University U.K. wishes to bring the concept of MEP knowledge along with architects together at one platform. Jamia Millia Islamia came into existence in1920 through the tireless efforts of its founders, such as Shaikhul Hind Maulana Mahmud Hasan, Maulana Muhammad Ali Jauhar, Hakim Ajmal Khan, Dr. Mukhtar Ahmad Ansari, Jenab Abdul Majeed Khwaja and Dr. Zakir Husain. It symbolizes the unflinching and resolute commitment of these great visionaries in bringing about socio-economic transformation of common masses, in general, and Muslims, in particular through the vehicle of education. The distinct identity of Jamia Millia Islamia has been eloquently explicated by Dr. Zakir Husain Sahib.. Authors are invited to contribute original research papers which can be submitted electronically via the conference website. The papers accepted after the review and presented in the conference will be included in the Proceedings of reputed publisher of quality standards.

    We cordially invite you to join us at ICEBU-2016, and look forward to welcoming you in Delhi, India!

    With best wishes Organizing Secretary
